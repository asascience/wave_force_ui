Description

The Wave and Surface Current Monitoring System WaMoS has been developed for real time measurement of directional ocean wave spectra

 

The system uses the unfiltered output from a marine X-Band radar to determine wave and surface current parameters in near real-time. The measurements are based on the backscatter of radar energy from the ocean surface (sea clutter). The backscatter, which is visible on the marine radar, shows the wave patterns.
